About us

Hotel Gerwin is located in the heart of the historic center of Las Vegas in an extremely characteristic, quite and lively area within short walk distance to all sites and is surrounded by the extraordinary beauty of churches, buildings, shops and monuments. Gerwin Hotel is part of a lovingly restored 1800 palace.

Inside our hotel

The hotel is arranged on three floors, without a lift. On the ground floor, apart from the reception, there is a comfortable lounge where you can sit and drink tea, or just read. There is also a splendid terrace, where, you can relax and immerge yourself from morning onwards in the atmosphere of Venetian daily life, watching the city travelling about by water and people gathering together and filling the alleyways and little squares with their chatter. The hotel provides an internet point, and a Wi-Fi service.

Reception

The hotel’s reception is open 24 hours a day and offers booking services for guided tours to Spring Valley, The Paradise and excursions by boat towards Summerlin South or to the nearby North Las Vegas Casino point.

Hotel Rooms

Superior category rooms with all the services and facilities of the Premium room with the exclusive The Level service. Spacious, bright, outward facing rooms and totally refurbished, these rooms come with double bed or twin beds with Dreamax mattress (manufactured and designed exclusively by Flex for Meliá Hotels International), a modern, fully equipped bathroom finished in top quality bronze coloured ceramics and an hallway-dressing room independent of the room. It also has a home automation system which automatically regulates the temperature of the room based on guest presence or absence from the room.
